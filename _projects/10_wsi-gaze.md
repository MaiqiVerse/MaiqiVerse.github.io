Bachelor's-thesis project at Northeastern University, China.

### Idea

Whole-slide pathology images (WSIs) are gigapixel-scale; exhaustive labeling is infeasible. We use **pathologist eye-tracking signals** as a weak supervisory cue to guide **efficient patch sampling**, then build weakly- and semi-supervised pipelines on top of those patches.
