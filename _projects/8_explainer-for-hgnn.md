[**ExplainerForHGNN**](https://github.com/MaiqiVerse/ExplainerForHGNN) is an open-source explainability benchmark for **heterogeneous GNNs**, released alongside our [MetaXplain](/projects/3_metaxplain/) study of meta-path attention faithfulness.

### Goals

- A common harness for view-factorized explanations on HGNN backbones.
- Schema-valid perturbations for faithfulness evaluation.
- Fusion-aware attribution that respects how meta-path views are combined.
- Reusable scaffolding for new explainers and new datasets.

Maintained as a community resource for explainability research on heterogeneous graphs.
