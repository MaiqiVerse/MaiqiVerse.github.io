**AutoGNR** uses **differentiable NAS** to automatically select heterogeneous aggregation paths and node-type combinations for graph learning — conceptually aligned with structured pruning and bi-level optimization.

### Key idea

A **non-recursive message-passing** scheme decouples path search from depth, improving search efficiency on large heterogeneous graphs.
